Safety and Containment Ratings

  • Blue → basically harmless / just put it in a cell
  • Green → minor risk / might “complain” a bit
  • Yellow → injuries possible / aggressive
  • Red → lethal / actively trying to escape
  • Purple → catastrophic / mass destruction potential
  • Grey → situational, depends on context

Identification

Basically, just how to search stuff.

Anomalies are added in order, and you can find them by searching "a" and the number. That is your Anomaly ID (ex. a1, a349).

Tests about anomalies are found by searching "t" and the number. That gives you a fresh Test Log of an anomaly (ex. t1, t210).

Researchers are just found with their name (warning: first letter must be uppercase!)
